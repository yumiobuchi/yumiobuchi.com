    Shape of stories

    Curious to test out Kurt Vonnegut's theory that "stories have shapes", this NLP project analyses sentiment progressions of 1000 literary texts.

    Naive Bayes is used for sentiment classification, and K-means clustering inspects whether books fall into typical emotional trajectories like “Rags to riches” (starts with negative sentiment, ends positive) and "Man in a hole"(positive,negative,positive).
